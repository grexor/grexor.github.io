 During my Phd I developed integrative approaches to study post-transcriptional modifications. We combined regulatory information with RNA-protein binding iCLIP data. In this way, we can visualize and represent regulatory information on exon/polyA site usage in relation to RNA-protein binding. You can read more about this and other work in these publications:

Gregor Rot, Zhen Wang, Ina Huppertz, Miha Modic, Tina Lenče, Martina Hallegger, Nejc Haberman, Tomaž Curk, Christian von Mering, Jernej Ule
High-resolution RNA maps suggest common principles of splicing and polyadenylation regulation by TDP-43

RNAmotifs: prediction of multivalent RNA motifs that control alternative splicing
Genome Biology (2014)

 In May 2008 I started working on several bioinformatics projects at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science in Ljubljana. We developed dictyExpress, probably the most advanced web application for gene expression profile analytics (clustering, GO enrichment analysis) at the time. I co-developed the first analysis pipeline for iCLIP RNA-protein binding data and started working on analysis of post-transcriptional modifications. Publications from this period include:

Julian Konig, Kathi Zarnack, Gregor Rot, Tomaž Curk, Melis Kayikci, Blaž Zupan, Daniel J Turner, Nicholas M Luscombe, & Jernej Ule
